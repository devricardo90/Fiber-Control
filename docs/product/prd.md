# PRD

## Product
Fiber Control

## Vision
Fiber Control is a business management platform for small recurring-service providers, especially rural internet businesses.

## Problem
Small providers often manage customers, recurring charges, field operations, and financial visibility through fragmented spreadsheets, informal routines, and disconnected tools.

## Goal
Provide a single platform to manage customers, recurring payments, financial overview, route planning, alerts, and business diagnostics with clear operational rules.

## Initial Users
- business owner
- finance operator
- customer support staff
- field operations staff

## Initial Scope
- customer management
- recurring payments
- financial overview
- route planning
- alerts
- business diagnostics
